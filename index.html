Amazing Features.

This platform has differenet unique features for the seller and buyers!

Presale of Agricultural product.

Another unique feature of the system is the sale percentage deduction. In this functional requirement the farmer can edit and manage their products in their shop and set a sale percentage deduction in their agri-product anytime.

Chat Overlay

This feature is exclusively for the feature users in order to have an overview on how the system really works, and to answer different queries of the target user of the system through chat overlay and messenger direct.

Top-up coins

Top-up coins serve as an electronic wallet to the farmer and buyer. User can add coins to their account if they prefer to do the cashless method of payments in the system.

E-marketing

This feature will help the farmers post their crops in the application for more chances to be seen by the buyers that browse the application. Pre-order- In this part of the system the farmer can already market their soon to be harvested product in order to have an assurance of future buyer before the harvesting season
